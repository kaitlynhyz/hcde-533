A1: Modelling and Laser Cutting!

Oct. 11, 2022

Requirements:

  • Kit consisting of at least 30 instances
  • At least one unique part
  • Parts must have slots that press fit into the other parts in the kit
  • Parts must be laser cut out on cardboard
  • Parts should fit and not come apart with a light shake test

Since I don't have any experience with laser cutting, this assignment was a bit intimidating and overwhelming. I decided to make my life easy and create simple shapes for the assignment

I started by sketching shapes that I would like to use and made a paper prototype using a dessert box.

Once I had settled on the design, I recreated it in Rhino

On the laser cutter, I used the following settings:

  • speed 40
  • power 100
  • freq 50
