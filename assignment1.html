Oct. 11, 2022

Here's my finished and assembled Press Fit Kit:

Assignment Requirements:

  • Kit consisting of at least 30 instances
  • At least one unique part
  • Parts must have slots that press fit into the other parts in the kit
  • Parts must be laser cut out on cardboard
  • Parts should fit and not come apart with a light shake test

Ideation

Since I don't have any experience with laser cutting, this assignment was a bit intimidating and overwhelming. I decided to make my life easy and create simple shapes for the assignment

I started by sketching shapes that I would like to use and made a paper prototype using a dessert box. I had to adjust the size of the prototype so the shapes aren't overlapping each other when fitted

Modeling

Once I had settled on the design, I recreated it in Rhino

Laser Cutting

Due to technical difficulties for the laser machines, I have not done the implementation part of the assignment. More information about my experience with laser cutting will be updated!
